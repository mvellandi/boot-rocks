About

This is an independent review of Boot.dev, a unique programming education platform that combines game-like elements with practical learning. I am Mario Vellandi, a marketing engineer who believes online education should be interactive and fun.

Why all the effort to make the video and website? Frankly, I love their platform and would like to help upskill more people around the world. The Boot business model and pricing is similar enough to a social entrepreneurship that in the long-term can:

  1. Radically demystify software development and logistics
  2. Reach far more people than universities or bootcamps
  3. Be far more effective than video courses alone

They and their teachers can impact far more peoples lives than universities, bootcamps, and video

Secondly, I wanted to refresh my video production skills after a 15-year break.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to learn new skills, we hope our review helps you make an informed decision about your programming education.
